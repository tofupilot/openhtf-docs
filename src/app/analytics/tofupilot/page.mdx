# Introduction to TofuPilot

Congratulations on writing effective test scripts with OpenHTF! However, to truly understand and improve your products, you need powerful analytics. Analytics are essential not just for technical insights, but also for driving sales and growth.

<Image src="/logo-tp.png" />

That is why [TofuPilot](https://tofupilot.com) exists! TofuPilot is a platform that seamlessly integrates with OpenHTF to provide comprehensive analytics for your testing data.

## Why TofuPilot

TofuPilot not only fills the analytics gap in OpenHTF but also provides:

- Seamless integration: Quickly connect and start using TofuPilot with your existing OpenHTF setup.
- Real-time data analysis: Get instant insights from your test data.
- Interactive dashboards: Tailor your analytics view to your specific needs.
